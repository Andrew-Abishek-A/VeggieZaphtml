About Us!

We intend to ease the supply chain from wholesaler to consumer through an agent called Zapper. VeggieZapp aims to do this with a subscription service that allows the consumers to order the required quanity of organic foods from the Zapper. The Zapper in return gets the aggregate requirements of his subscribers from the wholesaler. This helps to reduce the waste generated by quickly perishable foods and organic based foods as it sells in that day itself. The consumer can alter this amount on a weekly basis as per their needs. This enables zero wastage at the agent side compared to the current scenario where there is considerable wastage at a retailer. This could also reduce vegetable costs for consumers since the agent can sell vegetables and fruits at a lower price, as he does not incur loss due to food spoilage and wastage.

Social Responsibility

To manage and reduce food wastage.
